About

I'm a researcher at KU Leuven, working on computational linguistics and natural language processing. My current focus is on low-resource languages and what happens when you push standard NLP pipelines into territory they weren't designed for.

Outside the lab, I take photographs, mostly of light and architecture. I write occasionally — about research, about living in Belgium as an outsider, about things I notice and can't stop thinking about.

Research

My work involves parallel corpora, tokenisation, and machine translation for morphologically complex languages. I've worked with the VSC (Vlaams Supercomputing Center) infrastructure and contribute to open linguistic datasets.

This site

A place to collect things: technical notes that might be useful to someone else, photographs that felt worth keeping, essays that needed to be written. No particular editorial line — just things I'm interested in.

Feel free to reach out by email.
